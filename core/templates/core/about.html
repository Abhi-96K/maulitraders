Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Mauli Traders wasn't built in a day. It started in 2008 with a small shop and a big vision: to bring premium quality building materials to every doorstep in Marathwada.

Over the years, our commitment to transparency, fair pricing, and expert advice has turned first-time buyers into lifelong partners. We don't just sell cement and steel; we help you build your legacy.
